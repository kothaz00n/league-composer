## 2025-04-28 - [Optimizing getRecommendations Hot Loop with Pre-calculation]
**Learning:** In `src/engine/recommend.js`, the `getRecommendations` hot loop (iterating through all champions per tick) suffers a substantial O(N*P) complexity degradation because invariant computations—like determining the derived roles for a custom archetype (`deriveRolesFromPool`), checking for flex picks, mapping and constructing a normalized champion pool `Set`, and resolving specific roster flex role favorites—were being repeatedly executed inside the loop.
**Action:** Lift static or invariant calculations (e.g. `planNameExact`, `normalizedChampPoolForRole`, `customArchetypeDerivedRoles`, `flexEmptyRolesFavs`) out of the `getRecommendations` `for` loop to prevent unnecessary memory allocations, arrays scanning and garbage collection. This drastically improves baseline CPU performance per loop.
